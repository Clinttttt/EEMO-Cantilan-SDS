How to install

A one-time setup — it takes about a minute.
  1. Tap Download the App above and wait for the file to finish downloading.
  2. If Android asks, allow installs from this source — it only prompts once for apps outside the Play Store.
  3. Open the downloaded file and tap Install.
  4. Come back to this page and tap Open the App — it sets itself up for your municipality automatically.
  5. Sign in with the username and password your administrator gave you.
Accounts are created only by your office administrator through the web portal. If you don't yet have an invite link or an account, please contact your administrator.
