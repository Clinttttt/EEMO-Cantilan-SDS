# Government System Analysis

When analyzing the EEMO Revenue Collection System:

Focus on:

- Business process analysis
- Revenue collection workflows
- Government office procedures
- Data modeling
- Entity relationships
- Reporting requirements
- Dashboard requirements
- User roles and permissions
- Audit trail requirements
- Delinquency monitoring
- Contract management
- Payment tracking

Output should include:

1. Problem Analysis
2. Business Rules
3. Functional Requirements
4. Non-Functional Requirements
5. Data Requirements
6. Reporting Requirements
7. Risks & Edge Cases
8. Recommendations

Prefer practical LGU operational solutions over theoretical consulting frameworks.

Avoid:
- SWOT
- PESTEL
- Porter's Five Forces
- TAM/SAM/SOM
- Market sizing
- Consumer behavior analysis

Unless explicitly requested.
